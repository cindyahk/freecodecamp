    • ABOUT

    Cindy is a software developer from Mauritius, and currently living in the extremely populated Beijing. She is trilingual (English, French and Chinese).

    She came to Beijing, China in 2005 for her university studies and that was definitely a big challenge to study in Chinese language! After her graduate studies, she landed a software engineering job in Dell/Quest in Zhuhai, a gorgeous city near the seaside located to the South of China. She stayed there for two years until she moved back to Beijing.

    Progress is impossible without change.

    George Bernard Shaw

    She is now working in a French company TLScontact (part of Teleperformance Group), which works with embassies to provide visa application services. This was big change from working in normal IT companies, but she learnt many new things such as web/front end development. She can now write some cool stuffs with HTML, CSS, Jquery and has recently started learning UI frameworks such as Bootstrap. She has also grown to love using command lines in Linux.

    As for her hobbies, she loves cooking and baking, although she completely stinks at the latter. Measuring cups and spoons are definitely not her thing! She also enjoys gardening and learning about flower arrangements. In her free time, she sometimes colors her Secret Garden book and does some reading. Travelling is also one thing Cindy is a big fan of. Exploring and discovering new things are definitely her thing!
